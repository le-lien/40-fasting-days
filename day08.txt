FASTING DAY 8

RESURRECTION • REDEMPTION • HOLY SPIRIT

Introduction
Today we are not fasting merely to remember that Jesus rose from the dead. We are fasting to understand the power, the meaning, and the glory of His resurrection.

The resurrection is the greatest miracle in human history.

Jesus did not simply come back to life. His resurrection proved that He is truly the Son of God. It demonstrated that death has been defeated, that the sacrifice of the Cross was fully accepted by the Father, and that the powers of darkness do not have the final word.

The empty tomb proclaims that Jesus is alive.

The resurrection is God's answer to sin, death, fear, despair, and hopelessness. It is the proof that God's plan did not fail. Jesus lives today. He reigns as King. Through His resurrection, the way was opened for the Holy Spirit to come and dwell within His people.

Today we do not want to merely read about the resurrection.

We want to behold it.

We want to worship the risen King.

We want to hear His voice.

We want to receive fresh revelation.

And we want to glorify Jesus Christ.

---

Prayer Assignment for Today

1. Meditate on the Miracle of the Resurrection

Read the resurrection accounts in the Gospels.

Ask the Holy Spirit to reveal the greatness of this miracle to you.

Reflect on these questions:

- Why could the grave not hold Jesus?
- What does the resurrection prove about who Jesus is?
- Why is the resurrection the foundation of our faith?
- What does it mean that Jesus is alive today?

---

2. Thank Jesus for His Victory

Take time to thank Jesus intentionally for His victory over sin, death, hell, and the powers of darkness.

Thank Him:

- for the Cross,
- for the empty tomb,
- for forgiveness,
- for eternal life,
- for the hope that never dies,
- for His victory that became our victory.

---

3. Ask for Revelation

Pray:

"Lord Jesus, reveal to me the power of Your resurrection. Open my eyes to see what You truly accomplished. Let me understand the depth of Your victory and the greatness of Your Kingdom."

---

4. Welcome the Holy Spirit Anew

The resurrection led to Pentecost.

Ask the Holy Spirit to:

- fill you afresh,
- deepen your love for Jesus,
- renew your mind,
- strengthen your faith,
- empower you to live as a witness of Christ.

---

5. Glorify the Risen King

Spend time in worship.

Take your eyes off your problems and place them on Jesus.

He is alive.

He reigns.

He sits at the right hand of the Father.

He is King of kings and Lord of lords.

He is coming again.

---

Prayer for Day 8

Lord Jesus Christ,

I thank You that You are alive.

You did not remain in the grave. You conquered death and opened the way to the Father. Thank You that Your resurrection proves that Your sacrifice was perfect and that Your love is stronger than every power of darkness.

Today I ask You to reveal the power of Your resurrection to me. Fill me afresh with the Holy Spirit. Let my hope grow stronger, my faith become deeper, and my love for You increase.

Help me not only to know about You, but to truly know You as the risen King, the conqueror of death, the Lord of glory, and the Savior of the world.

May my life reflect Your victory.

May my heart burn with love for You.

May Your Spirit lead me into deeper truth and greater intimacy with the Father.

Jesus, You are worthy of all honor, all praise, all worship, and all glory.

Forever and ever.

Amen.

---

The Goal of Day 8

Today is not simply about believing that Jesus rose from the dead.

Today is about discovering why the resurrection changed everything.

The resurrection proves that:

✝️ The Cross worked.

👑 Jesus is King.

⚔️ Death has been defeated.

🕊️ The Holy Spirit has been given.

🔥 A new creation has begun.

And because Jesus lives,

we can truly live too.
