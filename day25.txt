DAY 25 – SPIRITUAL WARFARE PRAYER FOR MY HUSBAND

Calling Him Out of Every Grave into Resurrection Life

> "Jesus said to her, 'I am the resurrection and the life. The one who believes in Me will live, even though they die.'"
— John 11:25



Today we stand in faith and call my husband out of every spiritual grave, every place of darkness, hopelessness, bondage, fear, and death. Just as Jesus stood before Lazarus' tomb and commanded, "Lazarus, come out!", we declare that the same resurrection power of Christ is at work in his life today.

🙏 Prayer

Father, in the mighty name of Jesus Christ, I lift up my husband before Your throne.

I command every spirit of death, spiritual slumber, despair, rejection, fear, addiction, deception, sickness, and oppression to leave him now in Jesus' name.

I break every chain, every curse, every stronghold, every assignment of darkness, and every plan of the enemy over his life. Every prison door must open. Every grave clothes must fall off. Every yoke must be destroyed by the anointing of the Holy Spirit.

Jesus, call him out of every place where the enemy has tried to bury his destiny.

I speak LIFE over his spirit. I speak LIFE over his soul. I speak LIFE over his body. I speak LIFE over his mind. I speak LIFE over his heart. I speak LIFE over every dream, every gift, every calling, and every purpose You have prepared for him.

May every dead area come alive again by the power of Your Holy Spirit.

Restore his faith. Restore his passion for You. Restore his identity. Restore his strength. Restore his joy. Restore his purity. Restore his authority as a man of God.

Let every wall of darkness collapse before him. Let every chain be shattered. Let every hidden work of the enemy be exposed and destroyed by the light of Christ.

I cover my husband with the precious blood of Jesus. No weapon formed against him shall prosper. Every lying voice of the enemy is silenced, and only the voice of the Good Shepherd will guide him.

🔥 Declaration

Today I declare:

Lazarus, come out!

My husband will live and not die.

He belongs to Jesus Christ.

He is redeemed.

He is restored.

He is healed.

He is strengthened.

He is delivered.

He is filled with the Holy Spirit.

He walks in God's purpose, wisdom, holiness, courage, and victory.

The resurrection power that raised Jesus Christ from the dead is alive in him.

No grave can hold what God has called to live.

In the mighty name of Jesus Christ. Amen. 🙏🔥
