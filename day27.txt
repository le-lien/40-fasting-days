

We Pray for Our Children

> “Let the little children come to Me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of God belongs to such as these.” (Mark 10:14)



> “Children are a heritage from the Lord, offspring a reward from Him.” (Psalm 127:3)




---

📖 INTRODUCTION

Today we dedicate this day to praying for our children.

Children are not our possession—they are God's precious gift. He created them with purpose, knows their future, and has a unique calling for every one of them.

One of the greatest responsibilities of parents is not simply to raise successful children, but to lead them to Jesus. Children learn more from what they see than from what they hear. They imitate our attitudes, our words, our reactions, our faith, and our relationship with God.

Our homes should become places where children experience love, truth, prayer, forgiveness, wisdom, and the presence of the Holy Spirit.

Today we ask the Lord to raise up a generation that loves Jesus with all its heart.


---

🎯 TODAY'S ASSIGNMENT

❤️ 1. Bring every child to Jesus.

Pray for each child by name.

"Lord Jesus, I bring this child before You today. Lay Your hands upon them, bless them, protect them, and draw them close to Your heart."


---

🛡️ 2. Pray for protection.

Ask God to protect their body, soul, mind, and spirit.

Pray that He will guard them from harmful influences, temptation, fear, deception, and every plan of the enemy.


---

🌱 3. Pray for growth.

Pray that every child will grow in wisdom, character, faith, love, and obedience to God.

May they discover their identity in Christ and walk confidently in His purpose.


---

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 4. Pray for parents.

Ask God to fill parents with wisdom, patience, humility, and unconditional love.

Pray that they will lead by example, speak life over their children, and faithfully point them to Jesus every day.


---

💔 5. Pray for healing.

Ask Jesus to heal every emotional wound, fear, rejection, disappointment, trauma, and painful memory.

May His perfect peace fill every child's heart.


---

🔥 6. Pray for the Holy Spirit.

Ask the Holy Spirit to teach, comfort, guide, and strengthen every child.

May they learn to recognize God's voice from an early age and develop a deep relationship with Him.


---

👑 7. Declare God's promises.

Speak these declarations over them:

You belong to Jesus Christ.

You are deeply loved by God.

You are chosen and precious.

You are protected by the Lord.

You are called for His purpose.

The Holy Spirit will guide you.

You will grow in wisdom and favor.

God's good plan for your life will be fulfilled.



---

🏆 GOAL OF DAY 27

Today we pray that every child will grow up rooted in Christ, strengthened by God's love, protected by His hand, and equipped to fulfill His calling.

Lord Jesus, we place every child into Your loving hands. Bless them, protect them, heal them, and fill them with Your Holy Spirit. Raise up a generation that knows You, loves You, follows You, and boldly shines Your light in this world. Help parents faithfully lead their children to You every day. In Jesus' mighty name, Amen. 🙏❤️👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
