DAY 35 – STRATEGIC PRAYER ASSIGNMENT

Jesus Restores What Was Meant for His Service

> “The thief comes only to steal and kill and destroy; I have come that they may have life, and have it to the full.”
John 10:10


> “Now to each one the manifestation of the Spirit is given for the common good.”
1 Corinthians 12:7
---

📖 INTRODUCTION

Today is not primarily about material possessions.

Today is about what God has placed within you.

God created every believer with gifts, abilities, talents, ministries, and a unique calling.

The Holy Spirit gives spiritual gifts not for personal status, but to glorify God, build up His Church, and make His Kingdom visible.

That is exactly why the enemy attacks this area so fiercely.

When people never discover their gifts, stop using them, or become discouraged, much of God's purpose remains unfulfilled.

The enemy does not merely seek to wound people—he seeks to prevent them from serving.

He attacks joy, because joyful believers serve with passion.

He attacks peace, because peaceful believers bring hope.

He attacks relationships, because we are stronger together.

He attacks identity, so people forget who they are in Christ.

He attacks time, leaving no room for God's presence.

He attacks courage, so no one takes the first step.

He attacks love, so people give up.

He attacks faith, so no one expects great things from God.

He attacks revelation, so believers never discover the gifts the Holy Spirit has entrusted to them.

Today we are not only praying for restoration.

We are praying to become fully equipped and ready again for the work of God's Kingdom.


---

👑 1. ASK THE HOLY SPIRIT FOR REVELATION

Pray today:

Lord Jesus, show me everything You originally placed within my life. Reveal the gifts, callings, talents, and opportunities that have been lying dormant because of fear, wounds, disappointment, or discouragement. Open my eyes to see what You see in me.

Ask yourself:

Where did my joy begin to fade?

Where did I stop serving?

Where did I bury my first dreams?

Which gift am I no longer using?

Where has fear replaced faith?

Where have I become spiritually passive?

Where have excuses replaced obedience?

Which areas does God want to bring back to life?



---

🔥 2. IDENTIFY THE ENEMY'S ATTACKS

Ask God to reveal the areas where discouragement has gained influence.

Perhaps you struggle with:

Constant weariness

Hopelessness

Distraction

Fear

Feelings of inadequacy

Self-doubt

Fear of people

Past wounds

Unforgiveness

Feeling overwhelmed

Comparing yourself to others

Shame

Unbelief


Bring each of these areas honestly before Jesus.


---

⚔️ 3. PRAY FOR RESTORATION

Lord Jesus,

Restore my joy.

Ignite my love for You again.

Renew my faith.

Strengthen my courage.

Heal my heart.

Fill me afresh with Your Holy Spirit.

Teach me to serve again.

Open the doors You have prepared for me.

Show me my place in Your Kingdom.

Let my gifts bear lasting fruit.

Make my life a blessing to others.


---

🕊️ 4. ACTIVATE YOUR GIFTS

Gifts grow when they are used.

Serve wherever God places you today.

Your ministry may not begin on a platform.

It may begin:

In your family

In your home

At your workplace

In your church

With a simple prayer

By listening to someone

Through a word of encouragement

By visiting someone

Through faithfulness in small things


Every place can become a place of ministry when Christ is at the center.


---

📖 5. TODAY'S ASSIGNMENT

Ask the Holy Spirit to reveal at least five gifts or strengths God has given you.

Write them down.

Then ask:

How can I use this gift today?

Whom can I serve today?

Where does God want to use me?

What fear is still holding me back?


Read today:

John 10

1 Corinthians 12

Romans 12

Ephesians 4

2 Timothy 1


Ask God to renew your mind and equip you afresh for His service.


---

👑 PROCLAMATION

I am called by God.

I am equipped by God.

The Holy Spirit lives within me.

My gifts are given to build up others.

My calling belongs to Jesus Christ.

I choose to be faithful with everything God has entrusted to me.

My joy is returning.

My courage is returning.

My love is returning.

My ministry will bear fruit.

Jesus Christ is building His Kingdom, and I have the privilege of serving Him.

---

🙏 CLOSING PRAYER

Lord Jesus Christ, thank You that You have not only saved me but also called me. Thank You for every gift, every ability, and every opportunity You have entrusted to me. Forgive me for the times I stepped back because of fear, discouragement, or complacency. Restore my joy, strengthen my faith, and teach me to serve You faithfully in everyday life. Let my home, my workplace, my family, and my church become places where Your love is clearly seen. Fill me afresh with Your Holy Spirit and use my life for Your glory. In Jesus' name, Amen.
