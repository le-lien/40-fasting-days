⚔️🔥 DAY 1–3 – WORSHIP: LEARN TO WORSHIP GOD CORRECTLY

For the first three days, your assignment is not primarily to ask God for things, but to learn how to minister to Him. Many believers know how to pray, but few know how to truly worship. The Bible shows that thanksgiving, praise, honor, glory, blessing, and worship are not the same. Each reveals a different aspect of our relationship with God.

🙏 1. THANKSGIVING (Thank You for what You have done.)

Thanksgiving focuses on God's works.

Thank Him specifically for:

salvation,

forgiveness,

the cross,

His mercy,

His protection,

His provision,

His faithfulness,

answered prayers,

your family,

your life,

even the difficult seasons where He carried you.


Example: "Jesus, thank You for saving me. Thank You for Your blood that cleansed me. Thank You for never leaving me. Thank You for Your goodness every day."


---

👑 2. PRAISE (Celebrate who God is.)

Praise proclaims God's greatness.

Speak aloud:

You are mighty.

You are holy.

You are faithful.

You are righteous.

You are all-powerful.

You are my Shepherd.

You are my Deliverer.

Nothing is impossible for You.


Praise magnifies God above every circumstance.


---

🙌 3. WORSHIP (Give Him your heart.)

Worship goes deeper than words.

Here you simply love Him.

Tell Him:

I belong to You.

I desire only You.

You are enough for me.

I surrender my whole life to You.

Search my heart.

Let Your will be done in me.


Worship is about God Himself, not His gifts.


---

🏆 4. GIVE HIM GLORY

To glorify God means acknowledging that everything belongs to Him.

Declare:

Every victory belongs to You.

Every blessing comes from You.

My life exists for Your glory.

May Your name be lifted higher than mine.



---

👑 5. GIVE HIM HONOR

Honor means treating God as the highest authority.

Show honor by:

obeying Him,

listening to His voice,

approaching Him with reverence,

refusing distractions,

giving Him your full attention.


Tell Him: "Lord, You deserve the highest place in my life."


---

📯 6. MAGNIFY HIM

Magnifying God means making Him greater in your own heart than every fear, problem, sickness, or obstacle.

Declare:

Jesus is greater than my fears.

Jesus is greater than my circumstances.

Jesus is greater than every attack of the enemy.

Jesus is greater than death.

Jesus reigns forever.



---

📖 Practical Assignment

During these three days, spend 30–60 minutes each day with God.

Move through these steps:

1. 10 minutes – Thanksgiving


2. 10 minutes – Praise


3. 10–15 minutes – Worship


4. 5 minutes – Give Him glory and honor


5. 5–10 minutes – Sit quietly before Him. Listen. Write down what He speaks to your heart.



Read aloud:

Psalm 95

Psalm 100

Psalm 103

Psalm 145

Revelation 4

Revelation 5


Do not rush. Do not worry about saying the "right" words. Let your heart be fully focused on Jesus.

🔥 Remember

Many people seek God's hand (what He can give), but worship teaches us to seek God's face (who He is). When God becomes your greatest desire, your heart changes, your faith grows, and His presence transforms you. Everything else flows from that place of intimacy with Him.
