🙏 PRAYER ASSIGNMENT FOR TODAY

1. Read the Passion Story of Jesus (Matthew 26–28, Mark 14–16, Luke 22–24, John 18–20).

2. Thank Jesus for His suffering, His blood, and His love.

3. Ask the Holy Spirit to reveal the depth of the Cross.

4. Confess pride, rebellion, self-will, and sin.

5. Ask Jesus to crucify everything that opposes His will.

6. Speak the Blood of Jesus over your life, family, and future.

7. Pray for healing of body, soul, and emotions.

8. Ask God's Kingdom to reign in your heart, home, and daily life.

9. Take up your cross and choose obedience.

10. Stand in faith for freedom, breakthrough, and victory.

11. Spend time in silence before the Cross.

12. Listen to what Jesus wants to say to you.
