

> “Our fellowship is with the Father and with His Son, Jesus Christ.” (1 John 1:3)


> “The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, the love of God, and the fellowship of the Holy Spirit be with you all.” (2 Corinthians 13:14)

---

📖 INTRODUCTION

Fellowship with God is far more than prayer. It means inviting the Father, Jesus, and the Holy Spirit into every area of your life. God does not simply want you to believe in Him—He wants you to live with Him.

Jesus never lived independently of the Father. Everything He did flowed out of His fellowship with the Father and through the power of the Holy Spirit. This is the life you have also been called to live.

Fellowship is not built on emotions but on closeness. Closeness grows through time, attention, obedience, and trust.

Today is about seeing fellowship not as a religious duty, but as a daily lifestyle.

---

🎯 YOUR ASSIGNMENT FOR DAY 22

1. Examine your fellowship with God.

Answer these questions honestly:

How much time do I truly spend with God?

Do I only talk to God when I need something?

Do I also listen for His voice?

Which Person of the Trinity do I know the least?

What regularly hinders my fellowship with God?


Write your answers down honestly.

---

2. Spend one full hour with God today.

Divide your time intentionally:

15 minutes of worship

15 minutes of Bible reading

15 minutes of prayer

15 minutes of silence and listening


Pay attention to which part comes naturally and which part is most challenging.
---

3. Get to know each Person of the Trinity.

Spend intentional time with each One.

The Father

Thank Him for His love.

Ask:

“Father, what is on Your heart today?”

Jesus

Ask:

“Jesus, what do You want to show me today? What do You want to teach me?”

The Holy Spirit

Ask Him to lead you.

Pray:

“Holy Spirit, where are You leading me today? What do You want to transform in my life?”


---

4. Practice God's presence.

Throughout the day, intentionally invite God into your daily life.

Before every important decision, pause and ask:

Father, what is Your will?

Jesus, how would You respond?

Holy Spirit, lead me now.


Learn to live with God, not merely to work for God.


---

5. Listen more than you speak.

Spend at least 20 minutes in complete silence.

No phone.

No music.

No distractions.

Ask the Holy Spirit to quiet your heart.

Afterward, write down:

What thoughts came to mind?

Which Scriptures did God bring to your remembrance?

What impressions did you receive?


Test everything by the Word of God.


---

6. Pursue fellowship with the Body of Christ.

God never intended fellowship to be lived only individually.

Meet with another believer today.

Pray together.

Encourage one another.

Share what God has been teaching you.

Ask each other:

Where have you seen God at work this week?

What has God been teaching you?

What can you thank Him for today?



---

7. Remove whatever hinders fellowship.

Ask God to reveal:

Which habits steal my time with Him?

Which forms of media occupy my heart more than God's Word?

Is there any sin interrupting my fellowship with God?

Where am I relying on my own strength instead of His?


Make at least one practical decision today that will strengthen your fellowship with God in the long term.


---

🏆 GOAL OF DAY 22

The goal of today is to make fellowship with the Father, Jesus Christ, and the Holy Spirit a way of life.

Not only for a few minutes in the morning.

Not only on Sundays.

But throughout every part of your day.

Learn to live in His presence. Speak with Him. Listen to Him. Walk with Him. Share every part of your life with Him.

Spiritual maturity is not produced first by gaining more knowledge, but by living in continual fellowship with the Triune God. Those who remain in His presence will come to know His heart, recognize His voice, and be transformed more and more into His likeness.
