---

📖 Purpose of Today

God desires to speak not only to prophets, but to every one of His children. The Holy Spirit wants to teach you how to recognize His voice more clearly, understand His guidance, and walk in it throughout your daily life.

Prophecy is not primarily about predicting the future. In the New Testament, prophetic ministry is given to strengthen, encourage, comfort, and reveal the heart of God to His people (1 Corinthians 14:3).

Today's focus is to sharpen your spiritual senses. Ask the Holy Spirit to make your heart sensitive so that you can distinguish His voice from your own thoughts, emotions, fears, or other influences.

Remember: spiritual maturity is not measured by extraordinary experiences, but by intimacy with Jesus, obedience, humility, and faithfulness to His Word.

📖 Practice Today

Set aside 30–60 minutes alone with God.

Read the Scriptures slowly and ask the Holy Spirit:

> "Lord, what do You want to show me today?"



Write everything down.

Don't judge it immediately.

Don't rush to share it.

Receive.

Pray.

Test it.

Compare everything with Scripture.

Pay attention to:

Repeated thoughts

Deep peace

Bible verses

Pictures

Individual words

Names

Colors

Numbers (without overinterpreting them)

Symbols

Emotions inspired by the Holy Spirit

Impressions during worship

Impressions while praying for others



---

🔍 Test Every Revelation

Always ask yourself:

Does it agree with Scripture?

Does it glorify Jesus?

Does it produce love?

Does it lead to holiness?

Does it bring peace?

Does it build others up?

Does it exalt Christ rather than people?

Does it produce humility instead of pride?

Is it confirmed by the Holy Spirit?

Is this the right time to share it?

🙏 Closing Prayer

Holy Spirit, today I open my heart completely to You. Teach me to recognize Your voice with greater clarity and to understand Your guidance. Fill me with wisdom, revelation, discernment, humility, and love. Open my spiritual eyes to see what You want to reveal and my spiritual ears to hear what You are speaking. Protect me from every form of deception and teach me to test everything according to Your Word. May every prophetic gift in my life serve only to glorify Jesus, strengthen others, and build Your Kingdom. Lead me step by step into all truth, and help me remain sensitive to Your presence every day. In the mighty name of Jesus, Amen.

For deeper study today, take time to explore 20–30 different types of prophetic visions, revelations, and spiritual gifts.
