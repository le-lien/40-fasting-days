

Bring everything back under the Lordship of Christ

«“He is before all things, and in Him all things hold together.” (Colossians 1:17)»

«“All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to Me.” (Matthew 28:18)»

---

📖 INTRODUCTION

A kingdom remains at peace only when the rightful King sits on the throne.

Since the Fall, the enemy has sought to lead people away from God's rule. He not only steals peace but gradually takes ground in every area of life. He sows lies, fear, pride, idolatry, bitterness, and disobedience so that Christ no longer holds first place.

But Jesus came to restore what had been lost.

Today is not only about driving out the enemy—it is about giving Jesus His rightful place once again.

Where Jesus reigns, there is order.

Where Jesus reigns, there is peace.

Where Jesus reigns, there is truth.

Where Jesus reigns, there is freedom.

Every area of your life ultimately belongs to Him.

Today, you intentionally take these areas back and place them once again under the Lordship of Jesus Christ.

---

🎯 YOUR ASSIGNMENT FOR TODAY

Today, examine every area of your life.

Ask yourself honestly:

Who is truly sitting on the throne here—Jesus or something else?

Take your time and carefully go through each area.

👑 Declare aloud:

- Jesus, You are the Lord of my life.
- Jesus sits on the throne of my heart.
- I belong to Jesus Christ.
- My mind belongs to Jesus.
- My will belongs to Jesus.
- My family belongs to Jesus.
- My marriage belongs to Jesus.
- My children belong to Jesus.
- My home belongs to Jesus.
- My work belongs to Jesus.
- My finances belong to Jesus.
- My time belongs to Jesus.
- My gifts belong to Jesus.
- My future belongs to Jesus.
- My body belongs to Jesus.
- My mouth belongs to Jesus.
- My thoughts belong to Jesus.
- Every root of my life belongs to Jesus.

---

👑 Dethrone everything that does not belong to Jesus.

Ask God to reveal whether anything has taken His place:

- Fear
- Pride
- Fear of people
- Money
- Control
- Addiction
- Wounds
- Bitterness
- Religion without relationship
- Self-will
- Comfort
- False security

Confess these things before God and consciously choose to give Jesus first place.

---

👑 Proclaim God's reign.

Declare aloud with faith:

«“Jesus Christ is Lord over my life! No one else will sit on this throne. I belong to Jesus. My home belongs to Jesus. My family belongs to Jesus. Everything God has entrusted to me belongs to Jesus. May His will be done in my life.”»

---

👑 Put things back in order.

Ask the Holy Spirit to show you:

- What do I need to let go of?
- Where do I need to repent?
- Where do I need to forgive?
- What needs to come back under God's order?

Write everything down and intentionally bring it before Jesus.

---

👑 End the day with thanksgiving.

Thank Jesus for every area that you have placed under His Lordship today.

The enemy does not have the final word.

Your past does not have the final word.

Your feelings do not have the final word.

Jesus alone is King.

Where He reigns, restoration begins.

Where He reigns, God's order is restored.

Where He reigns, freedom becomes visible.

---

🏆 GOAL OF DAY 18

Not merely to drive the enemy out of the house,

but to intentionally place Jesus on the throne of every area of your life.

For when Christ reigns, His Kingdom comes.

And where His Kingdom is, darkness, chaos, and disorder must flee.
